What you need


  • a deck of cards
  • a flat surface to play on
  • someone to play against

How to play

Deal the cards evenly between the players

Any left over cards are stacked face up in the middle of the table

Players put their cards face down in front of them

Starting to the left of the dearler, players puts one card face up on the pile in the middle, without looking at the card

If the player plays a face card (King,Queen,Jack or Ace)then the next player must play a face down card

The number of tries the player has to get a face card depends on what face card the previous player player

If it is a jack they get one try

If it is a queen they get two tries

If it is a king they get three tries

If it is an ace they have four tries

If they do not get A face card within the tries Then the player who placed the face card gets the pile

If the player manages to get a face card then the next player must try to get a face card

Another way to get the pile is when two cards of the same value are at the top of the pile

When this happens the first player to put his hand on the pile gets the cards

The first person to have all the cards wins
