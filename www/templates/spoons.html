What you need


  • a deck of cards
  • a flat surface to play on
  • 3 to 13 players
  • One fewer than the number of players)

How to play

Place the spoons in a circle in the middle of the table


From the deck get all four cards of the required ranks (You need a rank for each player)


Then deal out these cards so there are four for each player


The players try to get four cards of the same rank


They do this by sliding one of their cards to the player to their right and taking the card given to them by the player to their left


Once a player has all four cards of a rank then they can take a spoon from the center


Once one player has taken a spoon all players can take a spoon (regardless of the cards they have)


One person won't be able to get a spoon and they will be given a letter


Then the cards are reshuffled and the next round starts


A player is out when they have spelt out S-P-O-O-N


The last player to be out wins
