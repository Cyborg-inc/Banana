What you need


  • a deck of cards
  • a flat surface to play on
  • someone to play against

How to play

Split the deck(s) equally between the player

Place one card face up and four cards face down

place one card face up,on top of the card face down that is adjacent to the card which is face up, and three cards face down

place one card face up,on top of the card face down that is adjacent to the card which is face up, and two cards face down

place one card face up,on top of the card face down that is adjacent to the card which is face up, and one card face down

place one card face up,on top of the card face down that is adjacent to the card which is face up, and place the remaining cards face down in a seperate pile(known as the spit cards)

You should have 5 piles of cards increasing from 1 to 5 in increments of 1 with the top card face up and all cards underneath face down(these are your stock piles)

when both players are ready they flip over the first of their spit cards

This creates the spit piles on to whcih player place cards from their stock piles

the players put their stock cards onto the spit piles if the the stock card has a value that is on above or one below that of the spit card

the image below explains the concept (you can go either way arounf the circle

if the player has a card face down then they can turn it face up and play it (if possible)

If the player finshes one of their stock piles they can move the face up card off one of their other piles to create a new pile

when one player gets rid of all their stock cards all players can hit one of the piles to claim the cards in that spit piles the player should go for the smaller pile

The players then take any remaining stock cards, their spit cards and the recently picked up spit piles and shuffle then

then repeat until someone runs out of cards

this can happen as eventually a player will have no cards to use for a spit card pile.

When this happens there will be a pile of no cards that a player can hit at the end of the round

playing with more than two people

when playing with more than two people the only things that change are

  • The number of spit piles
  • The number of spit card piles
  • The number of decks used
    • add an extra deck for every odd number of players (so 2 for 3, 3 for 5, 4 for 7 ,etc.
