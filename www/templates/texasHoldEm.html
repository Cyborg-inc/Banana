What you need

  • a deck of playing cards
  • something to bet with
  • someone to play against

How to play

players are dealt two cards face down


Each player can now exercise their betting options


Three cards are dealt face up on the table for all players to share

This known as the flop


The players have another round of betting


A fourth card is added to the flop

this is called the turn


another round of betting takes place


a fith card is dealt added to the flop

this is know as the river


a final round of betting takes place


when the betting has ended there will be showdown


Whoever has the best hand wins the pot
