What you need

  • a deck of cards
  • a flat surface to play on
  • 2 to 8 players

How to play

First take a card out of the deck (This is usually the queen of clubs)

Then deal out the cards among the players

Players then sort their hand searching for pairs and putting any piles they find face up in front of them

starting from the dealer, the player fans out his cards to the player to the left

The player must make sure that the other player does not see his hand

The other player must pick one of the cards from the player's hand and put it in their own hand

If they make a pair then they also put this pair face up on the table

This contiues around the circle of players until only the old maid (the one with no pair) is left

Whichever player is left with the old maid loses
