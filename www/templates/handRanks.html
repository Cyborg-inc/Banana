The hands of poker ranked highest to lowest are:

  • 1: Royal Flush

  • 2: Straight Flush

  • 3: Four of a Kind

  • 4: Full House

  • 5: Flush

  • 6: straight

  • 7: Three of a Kind

  • 8: Two Pairs

  • 9: A Pair

  • 10:High Card

Royal Flush

A Royal flush is an Ace-high straight flush
so an Ace-King-Queen-Jack-10 of the same suit

Straight Flush

A straight flush is a five-card stright of the same suit
So a 7-6-5-4-3 all of diamonds

Four of a Kind

Four of a Kind is four cards of equal value like 4 Jacks

Full House

A full house has a set of 3 of cards of one value and a pair of cards of another value
For example, three queens and a pair of twos

Flush

A flush is five cards of the same suit which can't be put in sequence

Straight

Five cards of sequnetial value made of multiple suits
for example, 7-6-5-4-3
intresting fact: all straights will contain a 5 or a 10

Three of a Kind

three of a kind is three cards of the same value, like three aces

two pairs

This is two cards of one and value and two cards of another value
For example, two jacks and two 8s

A Pair

one pair of cards of the same value like two queens

High Card

If none of the players have any of the above the palyer with the card of the highest value wins

In the event of a tie

In the event of a tie where not all 5 cards are used in the hand then the side cards(cards not being used) or "kickers" to decide the winner


The winner when using side cards is whoever has the highest valued card as their "kicker"


For example, if two players had and indentical hand of two tens and two fives but one player had a six and the other player had a four then the player with the six would win


If the players have and equal first kicker then a second,third or fourth kicker may come into play (it depends on what hand their is a tie on


if the when all the kickers are used then the pot is split between the players evenly
