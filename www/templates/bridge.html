What you need

  • a deck of cards
  • a flat surface to play on
  • 4 players

How to play

Bridge is maid up of two parts:


The Bidding


The play


Bridge is a partnership game so first form two teams of two


Each plyer should sit oposite there partner


A player then deals the deck of card evenly amongst the four players so each player has 13 cards

The Play

The objective of bridge play is to win tricks for your side


A trick consists of four cards (one form each player)


The first card to be played in each trick is the "lead"


The player who leads can play any card in their hand


After the lead the other three players have an obligation


They must play a card of the same suit if they have one


If they don't have a card of the same suit then they can play any card in their hand



After all four players have played one card the trick is complete


The rules for who wins the trick are simple


If the no trumps are played then the highest card of the suit of the lead wins


If any trumps are played then the highest card of trump suit wins

The Bidding

After looking at his cards each player in turn must make a call


A call is either a

pass, bid, double or redouble


Pass


If a player does not wish to bid, double or redouble they say 'pass'


If all players pass on their first turn then the deal is "passed out" The cards are taken in and re dealed by the same dealer


Bid


A bid is a statement to win a specific number of tricks with a specific suit as trumps (or with no trumps)


Each bib must say a number from 1-7 (add six to this number to get the number of tricks they plan to win) and the suit that wil be trumps (or notrump)


For example, if a player says 3 hearts they a bidding on winning nine tricks with hearts as the trup suit


Each sucessive bid must have either a higher number of trick or an equal number of tricks in a higher suit


The Suits in order of value are

  • 1st notrump
  • 2nd: Spades
  • 3rd: Hearts
  • 4th: Diamonds
  • 5th: Clubs

For example,

If the previous bid was 1 spade then the next bid must be either one 'notrump' or two of any suit or 'notrump'
