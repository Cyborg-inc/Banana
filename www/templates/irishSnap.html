What you need


  • a deck of cards
  • a flat surface to play on
  • people play against

How to play

Split the deck evenly amongst the players with the cards face down

Choose a player to go first

They will place a card face up in the middle and say 'one'

Then the next player will then place down acard face up a say 'two

This continues up to thirteen then the next player will place a card face up and say 'one'

this is repeated again and again

If when a card is put down it has the same value as the number said (Jack=11 Queen=12 King=13 Ace=1)Then all players must put their hands on the pile in the middle

The last player to put their hand in the middle must pick up the pile

this also happens if the two card placed in the middle has the same value as the card before it

whoever picked up the cards starts the next round

if a player says the wrong number or hits the pile when their isn't a snap has to pick up the center pile

When a player is out of cards they simply say the correct number when it becomes their turn without placing a card

The loser is the last person to still have cards
