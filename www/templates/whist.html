What you need


  • a deck of cards
  • a flat surface to play on
  • four players

How to play

The cards are ranked like this(top left = highest ; bottom right= lowest)

the four players make two fixed partnerships of two


partners sit opposite each other


A dealer is randomly selected


The dealer then deals out the card so everyone has 13 cards


The final card (which will belong to the dealer)is turned face up to show which suit is the trump


The turned trump will remain face-upon the table until it is the dealer's turn to play the first trick and may play any card


the other players play a car to that trick in clockwise order (following the suit if possible)


If the player can't follow the suit they may any card to discard or trump


The highest card of the suit that was lead wins the trick


unless their are trump cards played in whcih case the highest rank in the trump suit wins the trick


Play continues until all 13 cards are played then the score is recorded


How scoring works

The side that won more tricks gets one point for each trick in excess of six


So if a team has 8 tricks they score 2 points


The first team to score 5 points wins
