What you need


  • a deck of cards
  • a flat surface to play on
  • at least three players

How to play

Split the deck equally between the players


if there a remaining cards place them face down to start the discard pile


The player to the left of the dealer starts


The player puts down somecards of the same rank (or just one of that rank) and says the number of cards of that rank he has placed down


the next player then places down cards one rank above or below the previous player's rank


If the player has no cards of that rank they can lie and place down cards claiming them to be of that rank


this is repeated with the next player and so on


If a player believes that another is playing they can call out "cheat"


when someone calls cheat the person who is challenged must turn the cards they just played face up


if the challenged player was lying then he must pick up all the cards in the discard pile


however if the challanged player was telling the truth the challenger must pick up the cards


The winner is the first person to get rid of all their cards
